RayTracer is a robust 3D rendering engine implementing advanced features such as diffuse and mirror surfaces, indirect lighting, and efficient ray-mesh intersection using a Bounding Volume Hierarchy (BVH). Designed to produce high-quality and realistic visualizations, the project demonstrates the power of ray tracing techniques.

Features

  • Diffuse and Mirror Surfaces: Simulates realistic surface properties with support for reflections up to five bounces.
  • Indirect Lighting: Incorporates global illumination with recursive light bounces for enhanced realism.
  • Antialiasing: Reduces jagged edges by casting multiple rays per pixel and averaging results for smoother images.
  • Shadows: Implements direct lighting with shadows for realistic scene depth and clarity.
  • Ray-Mesh Intersection with BVH: Optimizes ray tracing performance by quickly eliminating non-intersecting objects in complex scenes.
